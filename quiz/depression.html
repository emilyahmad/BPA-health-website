Common Disorders - Depression

Below are concise, evidence-based overviews of several commonly experienced mental health disorders

Depression Disorders

Persistent sadness, loss of interest and changes in sleep and appetite

Overview and symptoms

What it is:Major depressive disorder and persistent depressive disorder involve low mood and functional impairment lasting weeks to months

Common symptoms:low energy, changes in sleep, appetite, feeling of worthlessness, difficulty concentrating, suicidal thoughts in severe cases.

Treatment & supportpsychotherapy, medication, antidepressants, peer support, and safety planning if suicidal thoughts are present.

When to seek help
